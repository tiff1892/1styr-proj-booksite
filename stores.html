Bookstores in the Philippines

Despite the fact that books can be easily read through mobile devices nowadays, there are still people who love the smell of a good old paperback. This is probably the reason why bookstores have started to emerge again in various parts of the metro. If you are still one of those rare “bibliophiles” around, then you have got to keep an eye on some of the top bookstores in the Philippines.

  • National Bookstore

    • Spanning to almost a hundred branches nationwide, National Bookstore or NBS has been a household name to every student and professional who are in dire need of any school or office supplies.

  • Fully Booked

    • Fully Booked is all about finding the next great read. Whether it’s Fiction or a beloved Memoir, great stories are everywhere if you take the time to look.

  • Goodwill / Bridges Bookstore

    • Goodwill Bookstore is one of the longest-running bookstores in the country as it was first established in 1938. The store is also known for its array of academic and reference books with a particular specialization on the field of medicine. As of now, Goodwill has branches in key cities around the Philippines including Davao and Cebu.

  • CentralBooks

    • Central Book Supply, Inc. has been in the publishing business since 1945. It is one of the most established publishers and distributors of law books and other professional books in the Philippines. Founded by Dr. Ernesto Y. Sibal, Central Book Supply had its first office and bookstore located at 769 Rizal Avenue, Sta. Cruz, Manila.

  • Power Books

    • Powerbooks is the pioneer specialty bookstore in the Philippines, carrying the most relevant selection of titles and catering to every book lover's tastes and needs.
