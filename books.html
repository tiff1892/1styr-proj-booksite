Different Types of Books

  • NON-FICTION

    • Non-fiction is a type of literature that gives information or describes real events, rather than telling a story. Biographies are examples of non-fiction works. Other examples of non-fiction books are the "how to" books and travel books.

  • FICTION

    • Fiction is the type of book or story that is written about imaginary characters and events and not based on real people and facts. It can also refer, more narrowly, to narratives written only in prose (the novel and short story), and is often used as a synonym for the novel.

    The two types of books can be further divided into many different sub-types, called "genres." Some of the books under those genres are even turned into movies. Others might not have been adapted into a movie or a TV series, but, they still captured the hearts of the readers. The best–selling novels of all time by very talented authors keep the readers on the edge of their seats, wanting to finish the book, but also do not want the book to finish.
