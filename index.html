Quotes About Books and Reading


  • “Reading is good. And you can quote me on that!”

    — STAN LEE, American comic book writer, editor, publisher, and producer


  • “Nothing is better than reading and gaining more and more knowledge.”

    — PROFESSOR STEPHEN HAWKING, English theoretical physicist, cosmologist, and author


  • “A reader lives a thousand lives before he dies... The man who never reads lives only once.”

    — GEORGE R.R. MARTIN, American novelist and short story writer, screenwriter, and television producer


  • “I like books that aren't just lovely but that have memories in themselves. Just like playing a song, picking up a book again that has memories can take you back to another place or another time.”

    — EMMA WATSON, English actress, model, bookworm and activist


  • “The images from the book you make in your head are always going to be the best images.”

    — RICK RIORDAN, American author



  • “I do believe something very magical can happen when you read a good book.”

    — J. K. ROWLING, British novelist, philanthropist, film producer, television producer and screenwriter
